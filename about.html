Our History

Emmanuel Fashion design was founded in 2007 by Titi Ademola, a fashion designer with Nigerian and Ghanaian heritage.
The brand emerged from Titi's passion for blending traditional African textiles and patterns with contemporary fashion trends.
Titi's educational background in fashion design from the London College of Fashion and her experience working with prominent designers in London shaped the unique aesthetic and quality of Fashion Design.
The brand quickly gained popularity for its vibrant prints, bold colors, and innovative designs, attracting a diverse clientele across Africa and internationally.

Our Vision

Emmanuel Clothing envisions being a leading fashion brand that celebrates African heritage and craftsmanship through modern and stylish clothing.
The brand aims to bridge the gap between traditional African designs and global fashion trends, making African-inspired fashion accessible and appealing to a broad audience.
By promoting sustainability and ethical practices, KIKI Clothing seeks to inspire a new generation of fashion enthusiasts who value cultural diversity, creativity, and responsible consumption.

Our Mission

The mission of Emmanuel Fashion Design is to create high-quality, stylish, and culturally enriched clothing that empowers individuals and celebrates African heritage.
The brand is committed to Combining traditional African textiles and motifs with contemporary fashion elements to create unique and trendy collections.
Ensuring meticulous attention to detail and high standards of quality in every piece, reflecting the rich cultural heritage of African craftsmanship.
Providing opportunities for local artisans and designers, especially women, to showcase their talents and contribute to the fashion industry.
